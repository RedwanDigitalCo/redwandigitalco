Why Digital Marketing Prices Vary So Much in Bangladesh

You've probably seen prices all over the place — someone offering SEO for BDT 2,000/month on Facebook, and another agency quoting BDT 40,000/month for the same thing. Both can't be right. Here's what actually drives the price difference:

  • Scope of work — a 5-keyword local SEO campaign and a 50-keyword national e-commerce campaign are completely different jobs
  • Agency size — large agencies in Gulshan charge premium rates for overhead costs; boutique agencies and specialists offer the same quality at lower cost
  • Tools used — proper SEO requires paid tools like Semrush, Ahrefs, or Screaming Frog that cost money, which professional agencies include in their pricing
  • Experience level — a newcomer charging BDT 2,000 and a specialist with a track record charging BDT 15,000 are not doing the same work
⚠️ Red Flag: If an agency or freelancer is offering full SEO services for under BDT 5,000/month, ask exactly what that includes. At that price, meaningful keyword research, on-page optimization, link building, and reporting simply cannot happen. You'll usually get a few meta tag edits and nothing else.

Facebook Ads Management Cost in Bangladesh

Facebook remains the dominant advertising platform in Bangladesh with over 72 million users. When budgeting for Facebook Ads, you're paying two separate things: the management fee to the agency, and the ad spend that goes directly to Facebook.

Budget Level Monthly Ad Spend Agency Fee Total Monthly Cost
Starter BDT 10,000–20,000 BDT 5,000–8,000 BDT 15,000–28,000
Growth BDT 30,000–60,000 BDT 8,000–15,000 BDT 38,000–75,000
Scale BDT 80,000–200,000 BDT 15,000–25,000 BDT 95,000–225,000

Facebook's cost-per-click (CPC) in Bangladesh is one of the cheapest in the world — typically BDT 5–20 per click depending on your audience and niche. This makes Facebook Ads exceptionally good value for local Dhaka businesses trying to generate leads fast.

⚠️ Watch out: Some "agencies" in Bangladesh charge a monthly fee but give you no access to your own ad account. Always insist on owning your Business Manager account. If they say no, walk away.

Google Ads (SEM) Cost in Bangladesh

Google Ads is more expensive per click than Facebook — but the intent is significantly higher. Someone clicking your Google Ad was actively searching for your service. Someone seeing your Facebook Ad was just scrolling.

  • Low-competition local keywords (e.g. "digital marketing agency Kafrul") — BDT 15–40 per click
  • Mid-competition service keywords (e.g. "SEO service Dhaka") — BDT 40–100 per click
  • High-competition sectors (finance, real estate, legal) — BDT 100–300+ per click
  • Agency management fee — BDT 8,000–20,000/month or 10–15% of spend

For most small businesses in Dhaka, a Google Ads starter budget of BDT 20,000–40,000/month in ad spend is enough to generate consistent leads, provided campaigns are set up and managed properly.

Content Writing & Blog Cost in Bangladesh

Blog content is what drives long-term SEO traffic. Prices vary significantly based on quality and experience:

  • Basic content (BDT 500–1,500/article) — usually thin, AI-written, or poorly researched. Risky after Google's 2026 core update which penalized thin AI content heavily.
  • Mid-range SEO content (BDT 2,000–5,000/article) — researched, properly structured, with real keyword strategy. This is the sweet spot for small businesses.
  • Premium expert content (BDT 5,000–12,000/article) — in-depth, original research, E-E-A-T optimized. Best for competitive industries.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does SEO cost per month in Bangladesh?

Monthly SEO packages in Bangladesh range from BDT 8,000 to BDT 50,000+ depending on scope. Local SEO for a small Dhaka business typically starts at BDT 8,000–15,000/month. National and e-commerce campaigns run higher. Avoid anything under BDT 5,000 — at that price, real SEO work isn't happening.

Is SEO or Google Ads better for small businesses in Dhaka?

Both serve different purposes. Google Ads gives you fast results — leads within days — but stops working the moment you stop paying. SEO is slower (3–6 months to see strong results) but builds a lasting asset that generates free traffic long-term. For most small businesses in Dhaka, the smartest approach is to run Google Ads for immediate leads while building SEO in parallel for long-term growth.

How much does Facebook Ads cost for a small local business in Kafrul or Mirpur?

A starter Facebook Ads campaign for a local Dhaka business can be effective with BDT 10,000–20,000/month in ad spend, plus an agency management fee of BDT 5,000–8,000/month. Facebook's CPC in Bangladesh is very affordable at BDT 5–20 per click, making it one of the best channels for local brand awareness and lead generation.

Should I hire a freelance SEO specialist or an SEO agency in Bangladesh?

Freelancers are cheaper (BDT 3,000–15,000/month) and can be great for single tasks like a one-time audit or on-page optimization. But for ongoing, multi-channel digital marketing, a boutique SEO agency provides better strategy, accountability, and consistency. If you're serious about growing your business online, an agency is usually the better long-term investment.

How long before I see results from SEO in Bangladesh?

For low-competition local keywords in Dhaka (like "SEO agency in Kafrul"), you can rank on page 1 within 4–8 weeks. For broader Dhaka-wide or national keywords, expect 3–6 months of consistent work before strong rankings appear. SEO is a compounding investment — the longer you run it, the better the results get.

A full-service digital marketing retainer covering SEO, social media management, and Facebook Ads management in Bangladesh typically ranges from BDT 20,000 to BDT 80,000/month depending on the channels included and the agency you choose.
