Why Website Design Prices Vary So Much in Bangladesh

Open any Facebook group for Bangladeshi businesses and you'll see web designers offering websites for BDT 3,000, while agencies quote BDT 80,000 for what sounds like the same thing. The confusion is real — and it costs business owners money when they choose wrong.

Here's what actually drives the price difference in Bangladesh's web design market:

  • Type of website — a 3-page static HTML brochure site and a 30-product WooCommerce store are completely different jobs, even if both are called "a website"
  • Custom design vs template — a premium pre-built theme customized to your brand and a fully hand-coded custom design require very different levels of skill and time
  • Included services — does the quote include hosting, domain, SEO setup, speed optimization, and ongoing support? Or just the raw design files?
  • Location of the agency — agencies in Gulshan and Banani charge premium rates to cover premium office rent. A boutique agency in Mirpur or Kafrul passes those savings on to you
  • Experience and portfolio — a first-year freelancer and an experienced developer with 50+ launched Bangladeshi sites are charging for very different levels of expertise
⚠️ Red Flag: If someone offers a "complete business website" for under BDT 5,000, ask for live examples of previous work and check if those sites are actually ranking or loading fast. At that price point, you're almost certainly getting a free template with your logo slapped on it — no SEO, no speed optimization, no real value for your business.

Understanding these factors helps you evaluate quotes properly. A BDT 40,000 website from an experienced Dhaka agency may deliver far better ROI than a BDT 7,000 website from a cheap freelancer that never ranks on Google, loads slowly, and needs replacing within a year.

Static Website Cost in Bangladesh (2026)

A static website is built with plain HTML, CSS, and sometimes a bit of JavaScript — no database, no CMS, no dynamic content. Pages are pre-built files that load lightning fast. This makes them ideal for professionals and small businesses that only need to showcase their services without frequent content updates.

Who is a static website right for? Freelancers, doctors, lawyers, tutors, small coaching centers, event planners, and any business that just needs a professional online presence without e-commerce or blog functionality.

📄 Basic Static Website — HTML/CSS Starter
BDT 8,000 – 20,000

What's typically included:

  • 3–6 pages (Home, About, Services, Contact)
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • WhatsApp chat button
  • Contact form (static, email-based)
  • Basic on-page SEO (title tags, meta descriptions)
  • Google Maps embed
  • Fast loading — no heavy CMS overhead

Best for: Freelancers, consultants, local service providers, coaching centers, clinics with no need for frequent content updates.

At the BDT 8,000–12,000 end, you're getting a clean, functional 3–4 page site. At BDT 15,000–20,000, you should expect a polished design, properly compressed images, fast load times, and a working contact form. Anything below BDT 8,000 for a static site in 2026 is almost certainly a free template with minimal customization.

⚠️ One important limitation: Static websites are harder to update yourself. Every change requires editing HTML files directly or going back to your developer. If you want to add a blog, update service pages regularly, or manage your own content — a WordPress site is a better investment.

WordPress Business Website Cost in Bangladesh (2026)

WordPress powers over 43% of all websites globally — and in Bangladesh, it's the most common platform for small and medium business websites. The reason is simple: WordPress gives you a full content management system (CMS), meaning you can update your own content without touching code.

WordPress website costs in Bangladesh vary significantly based on whether you're using a pre-built theme or a fully custom design:

🌐 WordPress Starter — Theme-Based Entry Level
BDT 20,000 – 35,000

What's typically included:

  • Premium theme purchase and setup (e.g. Astra, GeneratePress, Blocksy)
  • 6–10 pages customized to your brand
  • Mobile-responsive design
  • Basic SEO plugin setup (Yoast or RankMath)
  • Contact form and WhatsApp integration
  • Google Analytics setup
  • CMS training so you can update content yourself

Best for: Small shops, service businesses, restaurants, and startups in Dhaka wanting a professional online presence on a sensible budget.

⚡ WordPress Business Site — Custom Design Most Recommended
BDT 35,000 – 60,000

What's typically included:

  • Custom design (no generic templates — unique to your brand)
  • 10–20 pages including service detail pages
  • Full speed optimization — Core Web Vitals tuned for 90+ PageSpeed score
  • Complete on-page SEO for every page
  • Schema markup and structured data
  • Google Search Console + Analytics setup
  • Blog setup for content marketing
  • Security hardening (login protection, SSL, backups)
  • 3-month post-launch support

Best for: Established businesses in Mirpur and Dhaka that want a website that actually ranks on Google and drives real leads.

🏆 WordPress Premium — Multi-Location / Brand Advanced
BDT 60,000 – 100,000

What's typically included:

  • Fully custom-coded theme (no page builders)
  • 20+ pages including dedicated location pages
  • Advanced schema markup (LocalBusiness, FAQ, Service)
  • Bilingual Bangla/English content setup
  • Booking or appointment system integration
  • CRM integration (if needed)
  • 6-month post-launch support and monitoring

Best for: Multi-branch businesses, professional firms, healthcare providers, and any business in Dhaka competing seriously for online leads.

The most important thing to understand about WordPress website cost in Bangladesh: the design is only part of the job. A website that isn't speed-optimized won't rank. A website without proper SEO setup won't get found. Always ask your designer what their PageSpeed score targets are and whether on-page SEO is included in the quote.

E-Commerce Website Cost in Bangladesh (2026)

E-commerce is booming in Bangladesh. From boutique fashion stores in Mirpur to electronics sellers in Gulshan, more businesses are moving online. But an e-commerce website is a significantly more complex project than a business site — and pricing should reflect that.

Most e-commerce sites in Bangladesh are built on WooCommerce (WordPress's e-commerce plugin) because it integrates well with local payment gateways like bKash, Nagad, and Rocket, and local developers know it well.

🛒 Basic WooCommerce Store Starter Shop
BDT 50,000 – 80,000

What's typically included:

  • Up to 50 products set up and configured
  • bKash, Nagad, or card payment gateway integration
  • Product categories and filtering
  • Order management system
  • Mobile-optimized shopping experience
  • Basic SEO for product and category pages
  • Customer account and order tracking

Best for: Small online shops, boutique fashion sellers, and local businesses adding an online sales channel for the first time.

🚀 Custom E-Commerce Platform Growing Business
BDT 80,000 – 150,000

What's typically included:

  • Custom design with brand identity
  • 50–500+ products with bulk import
  • Multiple payment gateways (bKash, Nagad, card, COD)
  • Delivery charge zones by district or upazila
  • Advanced filtering: size, colour, brand, price
  • Full SEO optimization — product, category, and blog pages
  • Inventory management integration
  • Abandoned cart recovery
  • WhatsApp order confirmation integration
  • Speed optimization for Bangladesh's mobile-first users
  • 6-month support

Best for: Established e-commerce businesses in Bangladesh aiming to compete seriously and scale their online revenue.

⚠️ Critical for Bangladesh e-commerce: Always confirm your web designer has experience integrating bKash and Nagad payment gateways. These require merchant accounts and API approvals that take time — factor this into your timeline. A designer who has never done this integration before will cost you weeks of delays.

One more important note: e-commerce sites in Bangladesh must be genuinely fast on mobile. Over 80% of Bangladeshi online shoppers browse on mobile with average 4G speeds. A slow WooCommerce store loses customers before they even see your products. Speed optimization is not optional — it's core to your ROI.

What to Look for When Hiring a Web Designer in Dhaka

With hundreds of web designers advertising on Facebook and Fiverr, filtering good from bad is genuinely difficult. Here's what experienced business owners in Dhaka look for:

1. Live portfolio of real Bangladesh websites

Ask for live links — not screenshots. Visit those websites on your phone, check if they load fast, look professional, and actually rank on Google. If their past client sites are slow or look broken on mobile, yours will be too.

2. PageSpeed scores from previous projects

Ask them: "What PageSpeed scores do your sites typically achieve?" A good web designer targeting the Bangladesh market should be hitting 85+ on mobile PageSpeed Insights consistently. If they don't know what PageSpeed is, that's a major red flag for your SEO and user experience.

3. Local payment gateway experience

If you need e-commerce, confirm they've integrated bKash, Nagad, or SSLCommerz before — and ask for client references. These integrations are Bangladesh-specific and not all WordPress developers know how to do them correctly.

4. Clear scope of work in writing

Never proceed on a verbal agreement. Insist on a written scope that specifies: number of pages, revision rounds, what's included (hosting, domain, SEO, plugins), timeline milestones, and post-launch support terms.

5. Realistic timelines

A simple WordPress site can be built in 2–3 weeks with a focused designer. An e-commerce site typically takes 4–8 weeks. If someone promises a full e-commerce site in 3 days for BDT 5,000, they're selling you a template — not a custom site.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does website design cost in Bangladesh in 2026?

Website design costs in Bangladesh in 2026 range from BDT 8,000 to BDT 150,000+ depending on the type of site. A basic static HTML site runs BDT 8,000–20,000. A WordPress business site costs BDT 20,000–60,000. A full e-commerce store starts at BDT 50,000 and can exceed BDT 150,000 for custom features and a large product catalog.

How much does a WordPress website cost in Bangladesh?

A WordPress business website in Bangladesh typically costs BDT 20,000–60,000 for a professional result. Theme-based setups with customization run BDT 20,000–35,000. Fully custom-designed WordPress sites with proper SEO setup, speed optimization, and schema markup range from BDT 35,000 to BDT 60,000. Premium multi-location or advanced sites can reach BDT 100,000.

What is the cost of an e-commerce website in Bangladesh?

E-commerce websites in Bangladesh built on WooCommerce start from BDT 50,000 for a basic store with up to 50 products and bKash/Nagad payment integration. Fully custom e-commerce platforms with advanced filtering, delivery zones, and large product catalogs range from BDT 80,000 to BDT 150,000 or more depending on features and complexity.

How long does it take to build a website in Bangladesh?

A basic static site takes 1–2 weeks. A WordPress business site typically takes 2–4 weeks. A custom e-commerce site takes 4–8 weeks depending on product count and custom features. These timelines assume prompt feedback from the client — delays in providing content, logos, or revision feedback are the most common cause of project overruns.

What ongoing costs should I budget for after my website is built?

Budget BDT 15,000–40,000 per year in ongoing costs for a WordPress site: domain renewal (BDT 1,200–2,000), hosting (BDT 3,000–25,000 depending on quality), plugin licenses (BDT 2,000–10,000), and maintenance (BDT 5,000–15,000/year for updates, backups, and security). Ask your designer upfront whether any of these are included in their quote.
